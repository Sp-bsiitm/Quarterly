Financial Formulas

Return on Equity (ROE):

$$ ROE = \frac{Net\ Income}{Shareholder's\ Equity} \times 100 $$

Earnings Per Share (EPS):

$$ EPS = \frac{Net\ Income - Dividends}{Number\ of\ Shares} $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR):

$$ CAGR = \left( \frac{Ending\ Value}{Beginning\ Value} \right)^{\tfrac{1}{n}} - 1 $$
