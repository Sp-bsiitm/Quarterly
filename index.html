Valuation Model

We use the Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM) to assess investment risk and expected return.

The formula is:

$$ E(R_i) = R_f + \beta_i (E(R_m) - R_f) $$

  • \( E(R_i) \) = Expected return of the investment
  • \( R_f \) = Risk-free rate
  • \( \beta_i \) = Beta of the investment
